About

Resident Management System is a web application designed for ‘Housing colonies’ or ‘Society members’ or ‘Flats’ to make their colony idle by automating their day to day activities. The users in this system can post their service request in the system and they will get the service on time.

Residence Association

Caretakers

The assigned staff get requests from the users in the association and they process the request and send message to appropriate service providers around the area and posting common notifications from other area like power cut(Lift will not be available at this times),and water limiting in case of scarcity of water supplies or maintenance in supply lines etc.

Ladies Discussion Panel

The additional feature in this system is a discussion forum for the ladies in the society and this feature available only for ladies.
